How it works
Built for real merchants

Three steps from sign-up to collecting your first payment. No technical knowledge required.

📲
One QR. Every UPI app.
Customers on GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, or BHIM all scan the same code. On mobile, they tap directly into their preferred app. On desktop, they scan.
📬
Track your transaction by linking your banking email
Link your banking email once. UPAY watches for bank credit alerts via IMAP and marks transactions as settled the moment your bank sends the notification.
🛡️
Tamper-proof checkout URLs
Checkout pages use opaque token URLs. Amounts and merchant data resolve server-side — no way for a customer to edit the bill in their browser.
📊
Live transaction ledger
Every payment, timestamp, payer reference, and status in one dashboard. Filter by status, export records, flag disputes — all in real time.
