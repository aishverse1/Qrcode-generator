# UPIDirectPay

## Why does this exist?

In the current digital ecosystem, small businesses and independent merchants face significant friction when accepting online payments. Traditional payment gateways often impose substantial commission fees, settlement delays, and complex onboarding procedures.

UPIDirectPay was built to solve this problem by leveraging the Unified Payments Interface (UPI) network. 

We believe that peer-to-peer and merchant-to-merchant payments should be:
- **Direct & Instant**: Money should land straight into your bank account without intermediaries holding onto your funds.
- **Zero Commission**: You shouldn't have to sacrifice a percentage of your hard-earned revenue simply to process a transaction.
- **Frictionless**: Customers should be able to pay seamlessly using the apps they already trust (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM) without being forced through cumbersome web checkout flows.

By generating shareable payment links and dynamic QR codes that trigger UPI deep links directly on the user's mobile device, UPIDirectPay ensures that merchants can focus on their business instead of worrying about payment infrastructure.
